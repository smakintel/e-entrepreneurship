e-ENTREPRENUER COACHING PROGRAM

Emig Worldwide Sdn Bhd in collaboration with Novella Wellness Asset Bhd is committed to support the Malaysian workforce who have been affected by the pandemic and loss of income.

This program offers hope to all these candidates a program to achieve a brighter career path to realize their dreams.

  1. Understand how your Mind works.
  2. Build good Attitude to improve productivity.
  3. Form good habits.
  4. Understand and build better Self-Image.
  5. Set clear Goals.
  6. Understand the Law of Attraction and how it can help you.
  7. Design your Lifestyle

In 2021 we will be launching a 5-Day Workshop “Fishing Piranha in the Pandemic Ocean”, in reshaping your mind, providing new tools, driven by a SMART BAIT plan to overcome your current financial crisis and a sustainable income.
